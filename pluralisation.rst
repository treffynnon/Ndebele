Pluralisation
=============

People
------

Here follows a general rule to which there are inevitable exceptions:

+----------+--------+---------+---------+
| Type     | Prefix | Example | Meaning |
+==========+========+=========+=========+
| Plural   | o      | obaba   | fathers |
+----------+--------+---------+---------+
| Singular | u      | umfana  | a boy   |
+----------+--------+---------+---------+

Vocabulary: Simple Phrases
==========================

General
-------

yebo
   yes
hatshi
   no
hayi
   no (colloquial)

Greetings
---------

sakubona
   hello
unjani?
   how are you?
Sakubona Wendy, unjani?
   Hello Wendy, how are you?

   **Some suitable responses:**

   Ngiyaphila
      I'm fine
   Ngidanile
      I'm sad
   Ngithabile
      I'm happy

Ibizo lami nguSimon
   My name is Simon

Thanks
------

Ngiyabonga
   Thank you
Siyabonga
   We thank you
Sibongile
   We are thankful
